Srijan, started in 2008 with an aim to serve the rural community with providing support in terms of health, habitat and education based activities.

About

Our service towards serving the underprivileged community in rural areas of West Bengal helps families to get back to a normal life with proper hygiene and education to live with. All the projects we’ve completed till date have made us proud and have encouraged us for paving proper way of living to more families in the future.

Mission

Our mission is to provide sanitation & hygiene, clean
water and education for underprivileged.
Clean water supply

Providing installation of tube-wells and pumps for the supply of clean drinking water

Sanitation

Building sanitation system as septic tanks and urinals as part of Swatch Bharat Abhijan

Health check-up

Procuring diseases by organizing free health check-up camps

Education for children

Distributing books and study materials for free of cost to underprivileged children in order to help them to build a better future

Blood donation

Arranging open blood donation camps on querterly basis to serve the society with improved medical solution

Developing houses

Helping to build a better habitat through building houses for underprivileged in rural areas
